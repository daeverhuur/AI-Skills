## 1. First Impressions & Visual Impact (5-Second Test)

Navigate to the homepage or landing page. Evaluate within the first viewport:

- **Clarity**: Can a visitor understand what the site/business offers within 5 seconds?
- **Visual hierarchy**: Does the eye follow a clear path from most to least important?
- **Emotional tone**: Does the design convey the right feeling for the brand (trust, energy, calm, premium)?
- **Professionalism**: Does it look credible, modern, and intentionally designed (not templated)?
- **Differentiation**: Does it stand apart from competitors or feel generic?

Score: 1-5 for each criterion.

---

## 2. Hero Section Effectiveness

The hero section is the most important section on any page. Evaluate:

- **Headline impact**: Is it specific, benefit-driven, and immediately compelling?
- **Subheadline support**: Does it expand on the headline with additional context or value?
- **CTA clarity**: Is the primary call-to-action visible, clear, and action-oriented?
- **CTA contrast**: Does the button visually stand out from surrounding elements?
- **Imagery/media**: Does the hero image or video support the message and add value?
- **Layout composition**: Is there a clear relationship between text, image, and CTA?
- **Above-the-fold content**: Is the most important content visible without scrolling?

## 3. Typography & Readability

- **Font selection**: Are fonts appropriate for the brand? Distinctive or generic?
- **Font pairing**: Do heading and body fonts complement each other?
- **Heading hierarchy**: Clear H1 > H2 > H3 progression with distinct size/weight contrast?
- **Body text readability**: Is body text 16px+ with adequate line-height (1.4-1.6)?
- **Line length**: 50-75 characters for comfortable reading?
- **Text contrast**: Sufficient contrast against background (WCAG AA: 4.5:1)?
- **Text styling consistency**: Used consistently across similar elements?
- **Capitalization and spacing**: Uppercase, letter-spacing used intentionally?

---

## 4. Color & Visual Identity

- **Color palette cohesion**: Clear primary, secondary, and accent color?
- **Brand alignment**: Colors convey right message for brand/industry?
- **Accent color usage**: Distinct accent for CTAs and interactive elements?
- **Contrast and accessibility**: All text/background combos meet WCAG AA?
- **Color consistency**: Same colors for same purposes across site?
- **Background variation**: Varied enough to create visual interest between sections?
- **Gradient and effect usage**: Tasteful and purposeful if used?

---

## 5. Layout, Spacing & Visual Rhythm

- **Grid consistency**: Elements aligned to consistent grid or column structure?
- **Section spacing**: Consistent, adequate spacing between sections (40-80px typical)?
- **Element spacing**: Padding and margin consistent between similar elements?
- **Whitespace usage**: Enough breathing room, or cluttered?
- **Content width**: Body content constrained to readable max-width (900-1200px)?
- **Visual balance**: Page feels balanced, not too heavy on one side?
- **Section variety**: Sections alternate in layout (text-left/right, full-width/contained)?
- **Card and component consistency**: Cards/repeated components uniform in style?

---

## 6. Navigation & Page Structure

- **Navigation clarity**: Simple, clear, limited to 5-7 items max?
- **Logo placement**: Visible, links to homepage?
- **Navigation style**: Consistent with overall design?
- **Page structure logic**: Sections flow in logical narrative order?
- **Section identification**: Users immediately understand what each section is about?
- **Footer completeness**: Essential links, contact info, legal items?
- **Breadcrumbs/orientation**: User always knows where they are?
- **Scroll depth**: Appropriate page length?

---

## 7. Imagery & Media

- **Image quality**: High-resolution, crisp (2x for retina)?
- **Image relevance**: Support content and message, not just decoration?
- **Image style consistency**: Same style, color treatment, tone?
- **Custom vs stock**: Custom or authentic-feeling?
- **Image composition**: Well-cropped and composed?
- **Alt text**: Descriptive alt text for accessibility?
- **Icon consistency**: Same icon set, consistent style/weight?
- **Logo presentation**: Partner/client logos sized and aligned consistently?

---

## 8. Interactive Elements & Microinteractions

- **Button design**: Visually distinct with clear affordance?
- **Button hierarchy**: Clear primary/secondary distinction?
- **Hover states**: Visible hover/focus states on interactive elements?
- **Form design**: Fields styled consistently with clear labels?
- **Form validation**: Inline errors with helpful messages?
- **Link styling**: Links distinguishable from regular text?
- **Loading states**: Skeleton screens, spinners, or transitions during load?
- **Animations**: Subtle, purposeful, not distracting?

---

## 9. Responsive Design & Cross-Device Experience

Test at multiple breakpoints (desktop 1440px, tablet 768px, mobile 375px):

- **Breakpoint transitions**: Layouts adapt gracefully?
- **Mobile navigation**: Collapses to usable hamburger/drawer menu?
- **Touch targets**: Buttons/links at least 44x44px on mobile?
- **Typography scaling**: Font sizes reduce proportionally?
- **Image handling**: Resize or recompose for smaller screens?
- **Horizontal scrolling**: No unintended horizontal overflow?
- **Content priority**: Most important content prioritized on mobile?
- **Spacing adjustment**: Padding/margin reduced appropriately?

---

## 10. Trust, Social Proof & Credibility Signals

- **Testimonials**: Real testimonials with names, photos, company names?
- **Client/partner logos**: Credibility logos prominently displayed?
- **Case studies**: Detailed success stories with specific results?
- **Social proof placement**: Near conversion points (CTAs, forms)?
- **Team/about visibility**: Site feels human and trustworthy?
- **Security indicators**: SSL, privacy policy, trust badges?
- **Contact information**: Easy to find a way to contact the business?

## Scoring

### Per-Category Scoring (1-5)

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Excellent - Professional quality, no issues |
| 4 | Good - Minor improvements possible |
| 3 | Average - Several noticeable issues |
| 2 | Below average - Significant problems |
| 1 | Poor - Major redesign needed |

### Impact/Effort Framework for Recommendations

Score each finding:

- **Impact**: How much will this improve the user experience? (Low / Medium / High / Critical)
- **Effort**: How complex is the fix? (Quick fix / Moderate / Significant / Major redesign)
- **Priority**: Derived from impact vs effort

### Priority Categories

- **P1 - Quick Wins**: High impact, low effort. Implement immediately.
- **P2 - Strategic**: High impact, higher effort. Plan for next phase.
- **P3 - Polish**: Moderate impact. Address when time allows.
- **P4 - Backlog**: Low impact relative to effort. Consider for future.
